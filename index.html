The Zephyer Katara Hotel & Resort

GHM’s first property in Qatar, The Zephyer Katara Hotel & Resort mirrors the country’s spectacular dynamism while honouring its storied history. From first impressions of the splendid Mughal-inspired courtyard, The Zephyer Katara promises a one-of-a-kind experience to all who enter its graceful colonnades.

Overlooking the Arabian Sea, this modern architectural landmark bestows captivating vistas over a beguiling private beach and delivers an abundance of indulgences, from attentive, personalised service to exceptional international gastronomy matched by a sophisticated no-alcohol beverage programme.

Elevating the luxuries of Qatar to unprecedented excellence, The Zephyer Katara delivers Doha as its never been seen nor experienced before.

    The Restaurant

    The Restaurant

    Poolside with relaxing sea views, The Restaurant offers casual outdoor dining on its expansive patio for those who wish to dine with a side of sunshine, as well as an inviting indoor dining room under soaring coffered ceilings.

    Four inviting display kitchens serve Arabic, Indian, Japanese and Thai gourmet favourites as well as Western classics all day and into the night. A separate lounge is available for private gatherings.

    The Beach Restaurant

    The Beach Restaurant

    Clubby yet casual, this relaxed beachfront eatery welcomes all through its glazed French doors, opening onto a generous deck and the 35-meter-long family swimming pool.

    Modern European cuisine and the day’s freshest seafood taste even better just off the Josper grills.

    A large skylight above ensures a warm and sunny vibe from noon until night, when the time is right for innovative mocktails and favourite flavours off the Shisha menu.
